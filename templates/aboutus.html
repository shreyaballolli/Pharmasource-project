ABOUT US

A medicine donation can save lives and ease suffering when well-coordinated and managed. Good donation practices may provide savings in development support budgets,enabling these resources to be used for other purposes. This “PHARMASOURCE” application acts as a bridge between a huge medicine donation community and NGOs. A non-governmental association (NGO) is a not-incomedriven association that is free from states and all-inclusive administrative associations. They are ordinarily financed by blessings yet some avoid formal sponsoring totally and are run essentially by volunteers.In last one- or two-years users of smartphones have rapidly increased and counting is still on. It becomes very convenient for users to use the android app. As we know there are many existing medicine donation portals, they are not up to the marks and requires more manual data handling . By using this technology we are able to overcome the problems and provide a very user-friendly application for users..

OBJECTIVES

OBJECTIVE OF PROJECT Pharmasource is a way to collect unused medicines for further utilization by NGOs. The main objective of proposed system is:

  • To provide a direct bridge between donor and NGOs.
  • To reduce the wastage of unused medicines..
  • To provide a user-friendly interface.
  • To create a secured environment for the user interactions reduce manual data handling.
  • To provide a platform for donors to donate medicines.
  • To provide a platform for the needy to avail the required medicines.
